MARA JUNIOR SCIENCE COLLEGE(MJSC BP)

This is my secondary school which is MARA Junior Science College(MJSC) which is located at Balik Pulau, Penang. MARA Junior Science College (MJSC) (Malay: Maktab Rendah Sains MARA (MRSM)) is a group of boarding schools created by Majlis Amanah Rakyat (MARA), a Malaysian government agency. The institution provides learning facilities for bright students in local schools throughout Malaysia.I started my journey in MRSM right after my UPSR result came.At that moment, i was so scared to enter MRSM because it is a boarding school and I am afraid to be separate from my family but i have my English teacher to thank for which is Madam Shahdayanti, who motivated me to enter MRSM. Life as a boarding school students are very challenging, besides im still 13 years old.But, i admit that i have learn a lot of things in MRSM and the school have taught me to survive and be brave in defending myself.Furthermore,i also get many friends there, and we were like a normal teenagers at our age.
